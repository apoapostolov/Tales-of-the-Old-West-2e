### Montana territory history and chronology

2. `Montana.gov - Brief History`
   URL: `https://www.montana.gov/discover/brief_history`
   Status: materially used
   Use: broad Montana history framing, mining-era and territorial background

3. `Montana Historical Society / Capitol History`
   URL: `https://mths.mt.gov/education/Capitol/Capitol-History`
   Status: materially used
   Use: capital chronology, especially Bannack 1864, Virginia City 1865, Helena 1875

4. `Montana Historical Society / Governors`
   URL: `https://mhs.mt.gov/education/capitol/governors`
   Status: materially used
   Use: territorial governance context, Benjamin Potts, and the 1870 non-Indian population figure of 20,595

5. `Montana Historical Society / Livestock Brands Guide`
   URL: `https://mths.mt.gov/Research/Compass/Guides/Brands`
   Status: materially used
   Use: confirming that Montana began recording brands in 1873, which matters for cattle formalization and law

### Federal and historical landscape context

6. `National Park Service - Bozeman Trail`
   URL: `https://www.nps.gov/bica/learn/historyculture/bozeman-trail.htm`
   Status: materially used
   Use: route history, violence, and the relationship between access to Montana goldfields and contested country

7. `National Park Service - 7th Cavalry`
   URL: `https://www.nps.gov/libi/learn/historyculture/7th-cavalry.htm`
   Status: materially used
   Use: confirming the 1873 Yellowstone Expedition clashes and distinguishing them from the 1876 Great Sioux War

8. `Library of Congress blog - Mapping the Northern Pacific Railroad`
   URL: `https://blogs.loc.gov/maps/2022/09/mapping-the-northern-pacific-railroad/`
   Status: materially used
   Use: confirming that by 1873 the Northern Pacific had reached Bismarck, that Yellowstone surveys were active, and that 1873 railroad pressure was still survey/speculation pressure rather than built-rail dominance in Montana

9. `National Park Service - Grant-Kohrs Ranch / People`
   URL: `https://www.nps.gov/grko/learn/historyculture/people.htm`
   Status: materially used, lightly
   Use: Deer Lodge and cattle-country contextual support
