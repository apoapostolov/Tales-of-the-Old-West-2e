# Outlaw Bands - Tracker

## Rules

- Minimum final count: 25 active bands, target 30.
- Mark each band as `historical`, `composite`, or `fictional`.
- Do not let outlaw bands become interchangeable. Terrain, crime pattern, and social cover must differ.
- Every band needs at least one settlement tie and one enemy.
