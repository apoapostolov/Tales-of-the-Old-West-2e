# Montana 1873 - Outlaw Band Ledger

This is a working design ledger. Many names below are placeholders or composite handles pending source verification.

Wanted shorthand:

- `Local` local warrants or posse attention
- `Territorial` known across multiple counties
- `Federal` customs, Army, or marshal interest
- `Shadow` crimes known but not legally pinned

Hideout shorthand:

- `1` camp and move
- `2` reliable canyon or coulee bolt-hole
- `3` defended base with stores and remounts
- `4` networked refuge with town support

## Build Notes

- At least 8 of the final bands should be tied to freight or transport crime.
- At least 6 should be tied to stock theft or horse theft.
- At least 4 should function as quasi-political gunhands rather than classic robbers.
- At least 3 should have cross-border or federal implications.
- Every major settlement should have 3-5 linked bands within practical travel distance.
