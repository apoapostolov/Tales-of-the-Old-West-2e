# Deer Lodge

`Status:` Skeleton

Deer Lodge stands where valley agriculture, stock raising, courts, confinement, and road traffic all matter at once. It should feel less feverish than the big mining camps, but more rooted, more political, and in some ways more dangerous.

### Resource and Geology Notes

- Deer Lodge should be treated as a support-valley power center: hay, stock, provisions, courts, prison gravity, and road traffic tying mining and ranching country together.

### Factions in Deer Lodge

- Economic: ranchers, merchants, road contractors, prison suppliers
- Military / law: sheriff, judges, prison interests, deputies
- Native: route and valley diplomacy, scouts, displaced pressure from larger conflicts
- Political: courthouse machine, valley land interests
