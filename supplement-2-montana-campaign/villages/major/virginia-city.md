# Virginia City

`Status:` Skeleton

Virginia City, in the Alder Gulch country, is one of the old power centers of Montana Territory: rich in memory, poorer in easy gold than it once was, and still thick with men who made fortunes, stole them, or hanged rivals while calling it justice.

### Resource and Geology Notes

- Alder Gulch is the root of the town's existence. The settlement should be written as a place living off older discoveries, hardrock follow-ons, stored capital, and political memory rather than fresh innocence.

### Factions in Virginia City

- Economic: old mine owners, merchants, freight men, land speculators
- Military / law: sheriff, deputies, vigilante legacy men, territorial court hangers-on
- Native: indirect Crow and Shoshone frontier effects through routes and trade
- Political: ex-vigilante elite, courthouse bloc, territorial office seekers
