# Bannack

`Status:` Skeleton

Bannack is old Montana stripped down to the bone: a gold camp that once mattered enough to become the first territorial capital and still matters because decline never erases violence, grudges, or the men who know the rough country around it.

### Resource and Geology Notes

- Bannack should be written as a place where placer origins, hard country, and distance combine to make every remaining business harsher, meaner, and more personal.

### Factions in Bannack

- Economic: remnant mine owners, freighters, stock men, local merchants
- Military / law: sheriff, old vigilante lineages, informal armed cliques
- Native: indirect Beaverhead and upper-valley frontier pressure
- Political: old families, claim fixers, anti-tax holdouts
