# Fort Benton

`Status:` Skeleton

Fort Benton matters because the Missouri still matters. It is the northern freight throat of the territory, rich on robes, trade, whiskey, and imported goods, and tied as much to river timing and border traffic as to any mine.

### Resource and Geology Notes

- The town has river leverage, not deep local self-sufficiency. It depends on the Missouri, imported lumber and goods, and the violent economies of buffalo robes, freighting, and contraband.

### Factions in Fort Benton

- Economic: freighters, robe traders, warehouse men, whiskey traders
- Military / law: customs, Army patrols, marshals, hired guards
- Native: Blackfeet confederacy, Assiniboine, Gros Ventre trade and hostility zones
- Political: customs influence, territorial patrons, border profiteers
