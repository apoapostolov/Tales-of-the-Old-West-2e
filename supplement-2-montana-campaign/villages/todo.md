# Villages - Tracker

## Rules

- Every settlement file begins with a one-paragraph overview rooted in real terrain and economy.
- Every settlement has a statblock before hooks.
- Every settlement has faction presence notes.
- Every settlement has notable characters with usable motives.
- Major settlements get the full New Mexico rhythm.
- Moderate settlements get a compressed version unless promoted later.
