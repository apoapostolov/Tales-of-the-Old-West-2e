# Montana Village Template

Use this template for Montana settlement files when building a full village statblock.

The statblock format must follow the settlement rules in [corebook/08-campaigns-in-the-old-west.md](/home/apoapostolov/git-public/Tales-of-the-Old-West-2e/corebook/08-campaigns-in-the-old-west.md) and the presentation model in `Jornada Springs` from [corebook/09-the-new-mexico-campaign.md](/home/apoapostolov/git-public/Tales-of-the-Old-West-2e/corebook/09-the-new-mexico-campaign.md).

Do not invent a replacement statblock built from terrain tags. Montana material may add terrain, freight, winter, Native-political, or military notes in prose, but the village statblock itself should stay in the corebook format.
