# Stevensville

`Status:` Skeleton

Stevensville is a Bitterroot valley agricultural settlement carrying mission history, land transition, mixed authority, and a slower but no less serious frontier politics than the mining camps.

## Working Notes

- Settlement type: farm and mission-valley village
- Economy: crops, stock, mills, local trade
- Resource angle: water and valley land are the real wealth
- Hooks to build: deed disputes, church influence, horse theft, west-valley diplomacy
