# Philipsburg

`Status:` Skeleton

Philipsburg sits in the Flint Creek country where mining, timber, and valley support can still make a future if capital, labor, and transport hold together long enough.

## Working Notes

- Settlement type: active mining town
- Economy: lode work, mills, timber, ranch support
- Resource angle: stronger timber-and-water support than some gulch towns
- Hooks to build: labor shortage, assay fraud, timber wars, mountain winter isolation
