# Sun River Crossing

`Status:` Skeleton

Sun River Crossing is a logistics settlement first and a town second: hay, ferries or fords, wagons, stock, and fort traffic keeping it alive on a line that can turn from commerce to military crisis quickly.

## Working Notes

- Settlement type: crossing and service village
- Economy: freighting, hay, livestock, contract supply
- Resource angle: grass, water access, and road position outweigh mining
- Hooks to build: Blackfeet-country pressure, Army requisition, whiskey runs, freight theft
