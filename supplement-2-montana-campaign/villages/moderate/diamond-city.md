# Diamond City

`Status:` Skeleton

Diamond City is a Confederate Gulch survivor. The easy strike is gone, but enough money, washed gravel, bitter memory, and desperate men remain to keep the place dangerous.

## Working Notes

- Settlement type: declining mining town
- Economy: leftover placer work, salvage, supply, speculation
- Resource angle: water control and exhausted ground matter as much as ore
- Hooks to build: claim theft, hydraulic damage, old fortunes, ghost investors
