# Radersburg

`Status:` Skeleton

Radersburg is a thin-law South Boulder mining settlement where one good strike can remake reputations and one bad winter can strip the place to almost nothing.

## Working Notes

- Settlement type: mining village
- Economy: small lodes, local ranch support, freight dependency
- Resource angle: ore hopes outrun infrastructure
- Hooks to build: feuding claims, water scarcity, hired gunmen, election fraud
