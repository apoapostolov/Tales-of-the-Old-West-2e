# Argenta

`Status:` Skeleton

Argenta should be treated as a hard southwestern satellite: ore, remoteness, thin labor, and the constant question of whether this place has enough left in the rock to justify another season.

## Working Notes

- Settlement type: remote mining village
- Economy: silver and lead hopes, pack freight, local support trade
- Resource angle: distance and winter magnify every shortage
- Hooks to build: payroll robbery, mountain hideouts, exhausted ore, feud with Bannack interests
