# Companions, Bonds & Community — People as Pressure

> **STATUS: WORKSHOP MODULE.** Bonds, dependents, patrons, companions, crews, morale, communities, and shared recovery. Built from P10 inner fire, P13 settlement-as-character, AC2 relationship edges, and P7 upkeep.

## 1. Origin

- **Source patterns:** P7, P10, P13, P15.
- **Operators:** Domain Transfer + Fusion.
- **Target psychology:** attachment grants strength and creates risk.
- **Problem solved:** relationships should matter in the rules without becoming simple buffs.

## 2. Generic design space

A Bond is a named relationship with Trust, Need, and Strain. A Community is a shared Bond with functions and upkeep.

### Procedure

1. Name the Bond and what they need from the PC.
2. Set Trust 0-3 and Strain 0-3.
3. Invoke the Bond for +1 die or a recovery benefit; mark Strain.
4. Help the Bond in play to reduce Strain or raise Trust.
5. At Strain 3, roll Bond fallout.

### Bond fallout

| D6 | Fallout | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Distance | Trust −1 until a scene repairs it. |
| 2 | Demand | Bond asks for immediate help or payment. |
| 3 | Jealousy | Another Bond or faction is offended. |
| 4 | Danger | Threat targets the Bond. |
| 5 | Betrayal | Bond withholds aid or reveals a secret. |
| 6 | Break | Bond is lost until a project restores it. |

### Bond creation menu

Give every Bond one need, one gift, and one line they will not cross.

| Need | Gift | Line |
| --- | --- | --- |
| protection | shelter | will not harm innocents |
| truth | rumor/news | will not lie for you |
| money/material | tools/workspace | will not risk poverty |
| recognition | public support | will not be humiliated |
| freedom | safe passage | will not be owned |
| vengeance | violence | will not forgive easily |

### Invoke menu

Invoking a Bond marks Strain unless the action directly serves the Bond's Need.

| Invoke | Benefit | Cost |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **AID** | +1 die to a roll the Bond can help with | Strain +1 |
| **SHELTER** | safe recovery scene or hidden rest | Strain +1 or Supply cost |
| **TESTIMONY** | social leverage D6 | Strain +1; consumed if discredited |
| **RESOURCE** | tool, contact, minor funds, clue | Strain +1 or Debt +1 |
| **COURAGE** | clear Shaken/Stress once | Strain +1; Bond exposed |
| **SACRIFICE** | prevent a consequence from hitting PC | Strain +2 and immediate fallout check |

### Repair and deepen menu

| Action | Roll / requirement | Success |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **KEEP PROMISE** | fulfill a stated Need | Strain −1 or Trust +1 |
| **MAKE AMENDS** | apology plus cost | clear fallout tag |
| **SHARE DANGER** | Bond joins risky scene | Trust +1 if both survive |
| **PROVIDE** | spend resource/time | Strain −1 |
| **CONFESS** | reveal truth or secret | Trust +1, but create leverage |
| **LET GO** | accept Bond's boundary | prevents Break from becoming enemy |

### Community functions

A shared community has 2-4 functions. Each function can be invoked once per session/season and then needs upkeep.

| Function | Benefit | Upkeep pressure |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Hearth** | recovery +1 die | food/safety |
| **Workshop** | craft/repair access | materials/specialist |
| **Watch** | warning before threat | morale/arms |
| **Market** | buy/sell/scarcity relief | debt/tax |
| **Shrine** | stress/corruption recovery | taboo/service |
| **Network** | rumor, clue, safe passage | secrecy/favors |

### Bond design checks

- **Benefit check:** every Bond must be worth invoking.
- **Risk check:** Strain should create scenes, not passive guilt.
- **Boundary check:** a Bond with no line becomes equipment.
- **Spotlight check:** do not let one Bond solve all recovery, social, and resource needs.

### Bond archetypes

| Archetype | Trust starts | Gift | Need | Typical fallout |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Dependent** | 2 | emotional anchor, reason to continue | safety/provision | danger, demand, guilt |
| **Patron** | 1 | access, funds, authority | service/results | debt, withdrawal, scandal |
| **Comrade** | 2 | aid in danger, morale | loyalty/truth | injury, resentment, sacrifice |
| **Mentor** | 1 | training, lore, correction | respect/legacy | disappointment, hard lesson |
| **Rival-bond** | 0 | challenge, information | recognition | betrayal, duel, grudging aid |
| **Community** | 1 | functions, shelter, identity | upkeep/protection | unrest, demand, faction split |

### Trust and Strain procedure

Trust and Strain are separate because a relationship can be strong and burdened at the same time.

1. **Invoke:** mark Strain +1 and gain the benefit.
2. **Serve Need:** if a scene meaningfully serves the Bond's Need, reduce Strain by 1 or raise Trust by 1.
3. **Cross Line:** if the PC forces the Bond across its Line, roll fallout immediately at +1 severity.
4. **High Trust:** at Trust 3, once per session invoke without Strain if acting for the Bond's Need.
5. **High Strain:** at Strain 3, roll fallout; if Strain would rise above 3, reduce Trust instead.

### Bond event table

Roll when a session starts in a relationship-heavy game, when a Bond is neglected for a downtime cycle, or when Strain hits 3.

| D66 | Event | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 11-16 | **Small need.** | solve with time/resource or mark Strain. |
| 21-26 | **Useful news.** | Bond offers clue/rumor with a request attached. |
| 31-36 | **Jealous tie.** | another Bond/faction resents attention. |
| 41-46 | **Line tested.** | accept boundary or lose Trust. |
| 51-56 | **Threatened.** | Bond becomes target, hostage, debtor, or suspect. |
| 61-62 | **Opportunity.** | helping Bond unlocks function/training/access. |
| 63-64 | **Betrayal temptation.** | Bond can solve a problem by hurting someone else. |
| 65 | **Break point.** | relationship changes form: dependent leaves, patron calls debt, comrade quits. |
| 66 | **Sacrifice.** | Bond saves something precious at severe personal cost. |

### Community sheet

For a community-as-Bond, use:

- **Trust:** belief in the PCs.
- **Strain:** unmet needs and harm.
- **Functions:** 2-4 from the function list.
- **Need:** what the community lacks this season.
- **Line:** what it will not accept from the PCs.
- **Voice:** one NPC who speaks for it; one who disagrees.

### Worked mini-build

**Rebel cell:** Trust 1, Strain 1. Gift: safehouse and rumor network. Need: weapons and proof the cause is not doomed. Line: will not harm children. Invoke for shelter or false papers; Strain fallout brings informants, desperate recruits, or ideological schism.

## 3. Pressure loop

Need -> invoke relationship -> Strain -> help/neglect -> fallout or deeper Trust.

## 4. Choices

| Choice | Soft | Hard | Psychology |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Invoke | once/session | any time with Strain | warmth vs temptation |
| Fallout | mostly requests | severe danger/betrayal | drama vs tragedy |
| Community | background | sheet with functions | flavor vs campaign subject |

## 5. Integration points

Pairs with stress recovery, reputation, social combat, territory, and base play.

## 6. Handshake

- **Prerequisites:** named NPCs or community.
- **Inputs:** invoke benefit, recovery method, fallout severity.
- **Outputs:** Bond fields, Strain, Trust, community functions.
- **Touched systems:** recovery, social, base, GM threats.
- **Replaces or stacks:** replaces generic relationship bonuses.
- **Incompatibilities:** if host has relationship XP, decide whether Bond help grants XP or dice, not both.

## 7. Failure modes

Do not punish players for caring every time. Bonds need reliable benefits as well as vulnerability.

## 8. Check notes

Use `24 §7`. The worst case should create a rescue, reconciliation, or loss scene, not invisible penalty.

## 9. Publication handoff

**Player rule:** A Bond can help you when it matters. Calling on them costs Strain, and Strain must be repaired in play.

## 10. Worked example — Cozy/community

Each PC has two Bonds and one shared Home. Home functions grant recovery, crafting, or news. Seasonal events add Need; helping the Home clears Strain.
