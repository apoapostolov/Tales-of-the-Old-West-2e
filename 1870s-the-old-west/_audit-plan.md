### Chain B — The Bell / Ramsey gun-trouble arc (the outlaw thread)

A killing trouble in a saloon town — Bell rides for somebody; Ramsey is a man who came to kill him over a brother dead in Brownsville. The gunfire spills over.

- **Bell** — gunman riding for an unnamed boss
- **Ramsey** — comes for Bell over the brother killed in Brownsville
- **Marlow** — possibly the boss Bell rides for; arrives in town in #7 (Marlow/Kessel saloon)
- **Kessel** — German bartender, knew Marlow in another country
- **Bowman** — gets caught in the crossfire when the Bell trouble spills onto the street; this is the wound in #19
